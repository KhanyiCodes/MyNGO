Vilakazi Solutions is committed to providing sanitary towels and other essential toiletry for women and girls. In a bid to bring dignity to vulnerable women, a KwaZulu Natal woman is running a sanitary towel and toiletry donation initiative to improve the lives of those in need. Khanyisle Vilakazi is an extraordinary woman. She has given hope to young girls, she has given them the opportunity to be free, to participate in daily e-tivities, not to feel shamed or embarrassed, and has empowered them - all with a sanitary pad. They have their dignity back, through her initiative, Project Dignity. For many of us, buying sanitary pads is as easy as buying bread and milk. But this is not the case for millions of girls and women in South Africa. They are at a permanent disadvantage as they are forced to stay at home as they are unable to afford this basic necessity
