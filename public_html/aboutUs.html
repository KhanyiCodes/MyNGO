About Us

Khanyisile Vilakazi believes that women should come first


Khanyisile Vilakazi (25), of KwaMashu, works as a senior social worker at the Department of Social Development in Gauteng, and uses her own money, as well as donations from friends, family and other members of the community to purchase sanitary towels and other toiletry items that she donates to those who are in need. Speaking to SABC News, Vilakazi said she is pained to see fellow human beings, especially young women, facing the harsh realities of poverty and not being able to afford sanitary towels and other toiletries needed to practice proper hygiene, especially during menstruation. It is, therefore, her number one priority to lend a helping hand where she can. "I am a born leader and I have a heart for community development, and it pains me when I see a vulnerable woman exposed to poverty because of her background. I have dedicated my life to be a beacon of hope and to play a critical role in bringing change to the lives of my fellow humans," said Vilakazi.

History

Vilakazi Solutions was found by Khanyisile Vilakazi in 2018, in KwaZulu Natal , KwaMashu.

Mission

The main purpose of this project is to help provide teenage girls and young women in South Africa with sanitary towels.

Vision

Vilakazi Solutions plans to raise R200 000 to build a manufacturing plant that will produce affordable sanitary pads. The organaization will be targeting 100 female entrepreneurs to invest R100 000 each in exchange for shares in Vilakazi Solutions.
